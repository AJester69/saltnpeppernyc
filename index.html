The Story

Made to feel more like a warm midnight grill than a fast-food counter.

Salt n Pepper is built around the idea that comfort food can still have presence. The room is darker, the details are more considered, and the menu leans into richer finishes: aged cheese, pepper glaze, hot honey, char, smoke, and heat that lingers in the right way.

Instead of stacking the homepage with promotions, the new direction keeps the layout quieter and more confident, with a sharper focus on atmosphere, signature dishes, and a stronger visual identity.

"A premium comfort-food room with the appetite of a real NYC late-night spot."

  • Halal-friendly menu direction with burgers, chicken, fries, and shakes
  • Dark bronze palette, softer lighting, and fewer hard-sell prompts
  • Designed to feel elevated without losing the craveable fast-food edge
